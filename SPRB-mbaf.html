The Brief
01

A long-term packaging partnership built on honesty and craft.

Made by a Farmer is a Michigan-based cannabis brand built on craft and transparency. This is an ongoing packaging design partnership — not a single project. From rigid boxes to mylar bags, every SKU gets the same level of attention.

The flagship piece: a custom rigid box for their 1g Hash Rosin Brik Vape that functions as premium packaging and an in-store POP display simultaneously. One object. Two jobs. Neither one allowed to compromise the other.

The partnership has since expanded to include the Skinny V Zero gummies line and a 13-flavor joints lineup — each with its own packaging system that stays unmistakably MBAF.

Process
02
01
The Rigid Box
Rigid box design starts with engineering, not aesthetics. Built for two jobs — premium retail packaging and in-store POP display — simultaneously. The form had to be stable enough to display and premium enough to keep. Function led. Identity followed.
02
Mylar — Skinny V Zero
The partnership expanded into flexible packaging with the Skinny V Zero gummies line. Sugar-free THCV in a white mylar pouch. Warm coral and cream tones — a lighter visual language for a different product format while staying unmistakably MBAF.
03
Building a System
Each product line gets its own packaging expression — but they all live inside the same brand system. The visual language was built to scale. No rustic clichés, no borrowed equity. The brand is grounded, craft-forward, honest. The packaging reflects that every time.
04
13-Flavor Joints Line
Currently in production: packaging for a 13-flavor joints line. A new product format, same design foundation. When you build the visual language right from the start, it scales without breaking. That is the whole point of a system.
