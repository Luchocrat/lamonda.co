A sub-brand needs its own visual authority.

Lit Labs needed a line identity for their High Dose gummies that pushed harder than the parent brand. Not a variation — a sub-brand with enough visual force to own the shelf independently while remaining unmistakably Lit Labs.

The delivery format was mylar bags. The system had to work across Low Dose, Heavy Dose, Super Sour, Live Resin, and Liquid Diamonds. Five distinct lines, one coherent language.

The Bar System
A custom graphic element built specifically for this line — not borrowed from the parent brand. The bar system became the structural backbone of every SKU in the High Dose lineup.

Premium Finish Combination
Spot UV and metallic knockout applied to mylar. The combination creates depth photography cannot fully capture. You understand this packaging by holding it.

Super Sour — Hand Lettering
Super Sour became the platform for custom script lettering as the line identity. Hand-crafted type with metallic knockout — a SKU that commands the shelf without saying a word.

System Across Five Lines
Low Dose, Heavy Dose, Super Sour, Live Resin, Liquid Diamonds. Five product lines, each with its own personality, all speaking the same visual language.
